Meet Mike Marshall

From the Slopes
to the Classroom.

Before I was a teacher, I spent 30 years guiding raft trips and coaching ski racers at Mt. Bachelor. I learned that confidence is the fuel for any skill—whether skiing a black diamond or reading a difficult paragraph.

My journey into education started at home. I watched my own son struggle to learn to read, dealing with meltdowns and frustration. It was the Bend Learning Center that turned him into a confident learner. That transformation inspired me to earn my Masters in Teaching and dedicate my life to helping other kids find that same spark.

Relatable

I speak "kid" because I've been coaching them my whole life.

Experienced

Masters of Arts in Teaching (2010) & Licensed Oregon Teacher.
